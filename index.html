About

A passion that started at home

Hi, I'm Lucy — a 13-year-old Dallas native with a genuine love for organizing. What started as rearranging my own room turned into something I couldn't stop doing. I started noticing how much calmer a space felt when everything had a place, and I wanted to bring that feeling to other people's homes too.

I may not have a degree, but I have a real eye for detail, a natural gift for systems, and the energy and dedication to transform your space — one drawer at a time.

Every project gets my full attention and care. I work with you, listen to how you live, and create solutions that actually stick.
